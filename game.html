A weekly quantum strategy game

Quantum Channels

You hold a wavefunction over ten channels. Distribute your quanta, exploit superposition, and survive measurement, every week, against a real opponent.

Read the full rules (PDF) →

01

Distribute your quanta

You get 100 quanta to spread across 10 channels. Pile them up to win channels, but the universe doesn't reward greed.

02

Every week, a new quantum rule

Each week introduces a different quantum effect, measurement, exclusion, tunneling, uncertainty. The rule revealed only when the week opens.

03

Superposition token

Pick a channel you think you'll win. If you do, a coin flip grants you +0 or +3 bonus points. Lose the channel, no bonus.

04

Outscore the wavefunction

Out-bid your opponent in a channel? That's a point. Climb the global leaderboard, week by week.

Sign in to play

Create a member account or log in to submit your wavefunction each week. Game results, leaderboards, and your superposition tokens are tied to your account.
