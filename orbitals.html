Hydrogen Orbitals

Real wave functions of the hydrogenic atom. Drag to rotate, or let it spin.

red = ψ < 0   cyan = ψ > 0
Brightness ∝ |ψ|². Color shows the sign of ψ (real orbitals as used in chemistry). Built with WebGL volume ray-marching.
