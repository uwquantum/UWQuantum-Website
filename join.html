FAQ

A few questions students usually ask before signing up.

Do I need prior experience?

No. We welcome students of every level, from "I've never seen a wavefunction" all the way to graduate researchers. Talks and workshops are designed to bring everyone along.

Open to
All UW students

What's the Quantum Channels game?

A weekly browser-based strategy game where you distribute 100 quanta across 10 channels. Each week introduces a different quantum mechanics rule. Climb the leaderboard, learn the physics.

Play
Quantum Channels

Is membership free?

Yes. All events, the game, the Discord, and the orbital simulator are free for any registered student.

Cost
Free

How often does the club meet?

Biweekly on Wednesday evenings during the term. Drop-in talks, workshops, and time to hang out with other members.

Schedule
Biweekly Wednesdays
Time
7:00 PM

Why sign up?

The member portal lets you submit your weekly wavefunction in Quantum Channels, see your standing on the leaderboard, and get notified about upcoming events. It also lets us know who's coming.

Includes
Game, leaderboard, events

How do I stay updated?

Events are announced on our Discord and Instagram. Most weekly reminders go out on Discord, so dropping in there is the easiest way to keep up.
